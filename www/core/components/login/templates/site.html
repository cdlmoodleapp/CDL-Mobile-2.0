ABOUT OAUCDL

Established in 2002 by the Governing Council of Obafemi Awolowo University, our centre was charged solely with the responsibility to facilitate the development and co-ordinate the delivery of degree and sub-degree programmes by open and distance learning mode through the use of modern information and communication technologies.

The mission of OAU Distance Learning is to develop and provide through emerging network technologies, responsive and quality distance learning opportunities that meet diverse local, national and global needs.

OUR PROGRAMMES

The historical strength of our University lends itself to helping us develop programmes that are recognised for their excellence. Our programmes could provide you with the flexibility and qualifications you need to stay competitive in your career.

Some of our programme requires you to have previous qualification; as such you need to examine the programme you want study to ascertain its requirements. Choosing the right programme is crucial.


PREDEGREE

Our Pre-degree programme is designed to be an intensive coaching curriculum aimed at preparing senior high school graduates (senior secondary) for university education.Given the prevalence of deficiencies students carried over from secondary schools, our Pre-degree programme help students deal with the setbacks that often accompany such circumstances by providing them with a good head-start which will facilitate superior academic performance in the university.

JUPEB

JUPEB is a National Examination body saddled with the responsibility of conducting examinations for students, who have undergone approved subject combinations and are seeking Direct Entry admissions into Nigerian and partnering foreign tertiary institutions. The establishment of this examinations body was recently approved by the federal government of Nigeria to fill the gap created by the discontinuance of Advance Level Examinations.


DEGREE


Bachelor's Degree in Nursing Science

All Nursing Students will take core courses which provide knowledge in basic Nursing philosophy and practices; Elective courses in Law, Sociology and Computer Science are taken. As part of the academic programme, each Candidate would be required to go on six (6) weeks community and hospital postings in their 3rd and 4th sessions respectively. The Students would have designated Clinical Preceptors for this purpose they will be expected to have a seminar whereby they will share the experience they had throughout the period of posting.



Bachelor's Degree in Accounting

The specific objectives of the programme include the following the provision of basic knowledge needed for an understanding and analysis of problems of financial management of industrial, commercial, public and other human organizations,as well as the equipment of students with the skills needed for recognizing, defining problems and taking appropriate decisions, using scientific techniques and tools.



Bachelor's Degree in Economics

The specific objectives of the programme include Introduction to economic issues and basic principles and methods of microeconomics: how markets work, market efficiency and market failure, firm and consumer behavior, policy issues such as taxation, international trade, the environment, and the distribution of income.



POSTGRADUATE

Executive Masters Programmes are advanced level of master's degrees designed especially for mid-career executive professionals. These programmes are usually attended by full-time working professionals; therefore the programs are scheduled to match this condition. The OAU CDL Executive Masters Programmes have the eLearning advantage. With opportunities for online lectures, OAU-CDL Executive masters Students have the leverage of being part of an OAU standard teaching and learning experience without necessarily being adversely distracted from their normal day to day business.



CERTIFICATE
